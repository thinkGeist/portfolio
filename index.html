About Me

Hi there! I'm Nathan Geist, and this is my personal webspace for displaying my portfolio, resume, and whatever else may interest others. I'm currently in my fourth year studying at the University of Calgary for my Bachelor of Sciences with a major in Computer Science and a concentration in Information Security.
I have been interested in Computers and programming since a young age and started developing in C++ around 2009/2010. Since then I have developed a string liking for Python and C styled languages. I consider myself relatively comfortable in most languages such as: Java, C#, C++, PHP, HTML, Prolog, SQL (If that counts), and a few others. My end term goal is to be a full stack developer where I can hone my skills to develop both front-end and back-end systems.

I have substantial experience in Python due to my experience as a Python Developer for Hitachi ID Systems, I also have extensive knowledge of REST API's and have done work with ZeroMQ and messaging systems.
Please do not hesitate to contact me with any questions you may have as I'd be more than glad to discuss them or simply strike up a conversation about the industry. Also as this page is a work in progress and I apologize for incomplete sections. Thanks for taking the time to read this long winded paragraph, I hope to hear from you in the future.

- Nathan Geist
